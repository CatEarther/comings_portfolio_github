A Bit About Me

Ola! My name is Michael.
I'm a American-born Brazilian digital artist and graphic designer based in Lakeland who enjoys playing soccer while vibing to some good samba.

When I was 15 my father Gifted me a wacom tablet to begin my illustration adventures, I started off designing in GIMP but
have since progressed to be able to use every major Adobe program.
I am currently a student at Polk State College expanding my skillset and pursuing an AS degree in Digital Arts and Graphic Design.
I also enjoy creating art with Digital Photography, Astrophotography, and Tradicional Art mediums.
